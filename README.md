# SunJam Game
Repo for my FIU game jam project.

 ⚠️ IT HEAVILY LAGS WHEN STARTING THE GAME AND DURING MAJOR SCENE CHANGES!
About the Game

You are a Computer Science major, and because of your poor income and horrid connections, you have to find a bed at the local homeless shelter. Unfortunately, students from other majors (Art, Business, etc) have already taken all the beds (lol)!

Your goal is to reach the last available bed without dying!

You luckily find a flashlight key chain from a conference and a keyboard compressed duster. Gluing both together, you create an unconventional tool that'll help on your journey. Btw the bottle is turned upside down so it make a foam that you can jump on.

Controls

    WASD — Movement
    Space — Jump
    E — Toggle flashlight
    Left Click — Spawn foam
    Mouse — Aim flashlight

I made this in 24 hours for a University 24 hour game jam, and it's polished enough to submit as a Hack Club Project. Hope you enjoyed it.
